Bitey Beads

Babies love to chew, tug, grab and bite and that’s exactly what Bitey Beads are designed for: for Mom to wear and babies to chew.

Convenient

No more picking the teether off the dirty ground and no more broken jewelry to have repaired. Save your real jewelry for date night and wear Bitey Beads during the day!

Safe & Stylish

Bitey beads are strong, stylish and super safe. Made of 100% food grade silicone, BPA-free, Lead-free, Heavy Metals-free, Phthalates-free… they come in a variety of styles and colors so you can look great while keeping Baby happy.

Great for Teething Babies

The soft silicone beads add just enough pressure on babies gums to help relieve teething pain.

Great for Breastfeeding Mom's

Wear Bitey Beads jewelry while breastfeeding to give your baby something to keep their hands occupied (other than you!) This can help keep babies attention leading to longer feeding times.

Easy to Clean

Cleaning is simple. Just use soap & water or toss them into the dishwasher. Bitey Beads are dishwasher safe, making Mom’s life a little easier. :-)
